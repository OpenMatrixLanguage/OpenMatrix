Expressions and Statements

Statements are made of expressions, which may be simple or complex.

Many statements include loops, ifs, switches, try, breaks, and/or continues. For more info on loops, ifs, switches, try, breaks, and continues, see Flow of Control and Looping.

  • Expressions
    Expressions are groups of objects that can be combined into a single object. They typically involve literals, identifiers, and operators.
  • Statements
    A statement is a complete instruction to the OpenMatrix language. Statements typically combine keywords and expressions.
  • Comments
    Comments can be inserted in scripts.
