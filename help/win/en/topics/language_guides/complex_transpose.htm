Complex Transpose

Complex Transpose is different from transpose in that it takes the conjugate transpose of complex numbers.

When complex numbers are not used, it works the same as a normal transpose.

Transposes to a row vector. If any scalars within the vector are complex, the conjugate is returned rather than the original scalar. Transposes to a matrix. If any scalars within the vector are complex, the conjugate is returned rather than the original scalar.
